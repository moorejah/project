Military Spending Comparison

China has continued increase its military spending. Reasons include:

  • Point one: People's Liberation Army (PLA) is in the process of modernizing its ground forces so that they have the ability to contest a conflict with a "Fourth Generation" (4GW) adversary that is equiped with modern Intelligence Surveillance Reconnaisance (ISR) capabilities.

  • Point two People's Liberation Army Air Force (PLAAF) is in the process of upgrading its force and integrating "Fifith Generation" fighter aircraft and mastering over the horizon targeting.

  • Point three People's Liberation Army Navy (PLAN) is developing a "blue water navy" capable of projecting power far from the Chinese mainland and sistaining a force for an extended period of time.
